Your name is a lantern,
glowing inside my chest—
even in darkness,
I find the path toward you.

Your eyes are constellations,
I lose myself in their orbit;
every glance a universe,
every silence a song.

If the world should fade,
and time forgets its course,
still my heart will echo
only one truth: you.

Forever,
Doyoung
